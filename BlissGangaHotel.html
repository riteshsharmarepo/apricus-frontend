Bliss Ganga Hotel

Bliss Ganga Hotel conveniently located in Tapovan area of the Yogh Nagri of Uttarakhand in the Shivalik Hills of Himalayan Belt. The Bliss Ganga is the ideal place for leisure, recreation and business. The hotel is just 10 minutes from the Rishikesh Railway Station, and about 30 minutes away from the Jolly Grant Airport. National highways and state highways connect major cities.

Bliss Ganga offers 46 well-decorated rooms to the guests. Each room is well appointed with modern facilities to suit all your needs like in-room Tea/Coffee maker, Electronic safe, Mini Fridge, Writing desk, direct dial telephone, Wi-Fi, and Round-the-clock Room service, making it ideal for both business and leisure travelers.

We also offer exceptional amenities, including a Swimming Pool, Banquet Hall, and Restaurant, providing a level of comfort and style not found at other area hotels.

Laxman Jhula

500 Mtr from Hotel

Lakshman Jhula is a suspension bridge across the river Ganges, located 5 kilometres north-east of the city of Rishikesh in the Indian state of Uttarakhand. The bridge connects the two villages of Tapovan in Tehri Garhwal district, on the west bank of the river, to Jonk in Pauri Garhwal district, on the east bank.
